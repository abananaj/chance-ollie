Annual Fund – Donor Benefits

Contributors play a huge part in Chance Theater’s success. In appreciation of their generous support, we offer a variety of benefits designed to bring you closer to the artists and the work. For more information, please contact Erika Miller at (657) 549-0499 or members@ChanceTheater.com.

Join the Producer’s Circle or become a Program Sponsor and get more exclusive benefits like access to the dress rehearsals and much more! For more details, contact Erika C. Miller at (888) 455‐4212 or email Erika@ChanceTheater.com to send you a packet.

Program Sponsorships

Artists Fund

Help significantly increase the support to our theater artists, including directors, designers, actors, stage managers and equity contracts.

Our production artists provide an extraordinarily large subsidy to the Chance and we aim to make a meaningful contribution in hopes of offsetting this.

  • Recognized at Season Bash
  • Receive a signed copy of one script
  • Access to attend one First Read-Through
  • Complimentary Director’s Dinner tickets to one show with a guest (FMV* $80)
  • Attend One Closing Party

New Works Fund

Help create a home for the hottest new voices in American Theater through our new works program, On The Radar (OTR).

Many of the plays and musicals featured in OTR have gone on to full productions in major theaters across the country, including Lincoln Center, Manhattan Theatre Club and our very own.

  • Receive a signed copy of one script
  • Attend Playwright Reception with a guest
  • Attend Post-Reading Receptions
  • Recognized at all OTR performances
  • Attend Playwright’s Lunch (FMV* $60)

Education & Outreach Fund

Help to provide full scholarships to our summer youth workshop students, bring young adults to the theater through ticket sponsorships, provide free performances in the community, support other local non‐profits through ticket donations, and make theater accessible for all families through our Relaxed Performance initiative.

  • Attend Speak Up student workshops
  • Attend Speak Up Tech Lunch with a guest (FMV* $20)
  • Observe Speak Up rehearsals
  • Attend Speak Up Reception
  • Recognized at all Speak Up performances

Shared Annual Fund Benefits

When you support Chance Theater as a producer, you become publicly affiliated with a theater company who is receiving both local and regional attention. While you enjoy getting up close and personal with the Chance, you are also providing vital support for the artists, the productions, the organization, and your community. For more information, please contact Erika Miller at (888) 455‐4212 or Erika@ChanceTheater.com

*FMV = The Fair Market Value (A good faith estimate of the value of the benefits received.)
